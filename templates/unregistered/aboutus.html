About AttendAI

We're revolutionizing attendance management with AI-powered solutions that make tracking, reporting, and analytics seamless for educational institutions and businesses worldwide.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, AttendAI began as a student project at a leading technology university. Our team recognized the inefficiencies in traditional attendance tracking systems and set out to create a smarter solution. Today, we serve over 500 institutions across 30 countries, helping them save thousands of hours annually while improving accuracy and insights.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To revolutionize attendance management through innovative AI technology, making it effortless for organizations to track, analyze, and optimize participation while providing actionable insights for better decision-making.

Our Vision

To become the global standard in attendance management systems, empowering every educational and corporate institution with smart, seamless, and scalable solutions that enhance productivity and engagement.
